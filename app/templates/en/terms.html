1. General Information

General Knowledge Bot is a Discord bot for general knowledge. It offers several commands and features to test your knowledge with the members of your server. To see how to install and use its commands, visit our homepage. Every time a member uses a test command, they earn points if they answer correctly and lose points if they answer incorrectly. This information, along with other data, is stored in a database. To learn more, check out our privacy policy.
